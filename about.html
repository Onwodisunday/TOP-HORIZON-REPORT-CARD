Our History

Top Horizon Nursery and Primary School was established with a vision to provide world-class education in a serene and conducive environment. Since our inception, we have grown from a modest beginning to become a beacon of academic excellence in Lagos.

We have consistently produced pupils who excel in their secondary education and beyond, bearing the hallmark of discipline and intelligence.

Our Mission

To nurture total children who are morally sound, academically excellent, and socially responsible.
